The program runs a stop motion animation booth for a kids fair.
Hardware: 
- Windows 11 desktop (HP optiplex sff 5050)
- webcam (currently Diccik 1080p but looking to upgrade)
- arduino connected to 4 or 5 momentary push buttons

I expect the software to allow a child with little knowledge or attention 
span to be able to make simple videos.
the green button will take a picture.
the red button will delete the last picture taken.
the blue button will make the movie and display it to them
the white and black buttons will act as answers to yes/no questions-
for instance "save this movie and upload to cloud for storage (white) or 
continue working (black)"
the yellow button will turn onion skinning on and off.
